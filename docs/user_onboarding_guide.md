- Tone: Friendly

### Making the Most of Your First Chat

1. **Start Simple**: Begin with a clear, straightforward message
2. **Provide Context**: Mention relevant background information
3. **Be Specific**: The more details you provide, the better the response
4. **Ask Follow-ups**: Don't hesitate to ask for clarification or expansion
5. **Experiment**: Try different tones and settings to see what works best

### Understanding AI Responses

**Response Elements:**
- **Main Response**: The primary answer to your message
- **Confidence Score**: How certain the AI is about its response (0.0-1.0)
- **Emotion Detection**: What emotional context the AI identified
- **Suggested Actions**: Practical next steps you might consider
- **Follow-up Questions**: Ways to continue the conversation

**Quality Indicators:**
- **High Confidence (0.8-1.0)**: Response is very reliable
- **Medium Confidence (0.6-0.8)**: Generally good, minor verification recommended
- **Low Confidence (0.0-0.6)**: Consider regenerating or providing more context

  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
